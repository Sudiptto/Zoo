White Rhino

The White Rhino is one of the largest land mammals, native to Africa. They have two horns on their snouts, with the front horn being larger. White Rhinos are known for their wide mouths, which they use for grazing on grass. Despite their size, they can run surprisingly fast, reaching speeds of up to 30 miles per hour. These gentle giants are social animals, often seen in groups called crashes.
