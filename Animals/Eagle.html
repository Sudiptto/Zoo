Bald Eagle

The Bald Eagle is a bird of prey found in North America. It is the national bird and symbol of the United States of America. Bald Eagles are known for their white heads and tails, which contrast with their dark brown bodies and wings. These majestic birds primarily feed on fish, which they catch with their sharp talons. Bald Eagles are powerful and can reach speeds of up to 40 miles per hour while in the air!
