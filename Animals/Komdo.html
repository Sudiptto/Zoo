Komodo Dragon

The Komodo dragon is the largest living species of lizard, growing up to 10 feet in length and weighing around 150 pounds. Native to the Indonesian islands, these predators have a strong sense of smell, which they use to locate prey from miles away. Komodo dragons have a venomous bite, with toxins that can cause paralysis, blood loss, and shock in their prey. Despite their size, they can run at speeds of up to 13 miles per hour. You would not want to bear one!
